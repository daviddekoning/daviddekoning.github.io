Here are the ingredients by weight, not volume:

- 170 g all purpose flour
- 15 g Baking Powder
- 5 g salt
- 50 g sugar
- 340 g milk
- 2 whole egg yolks
- 14 g vanilla extract
- 113 g salted butter
- 4 whole egg whites

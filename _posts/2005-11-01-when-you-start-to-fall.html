Terrifying feeling, but it's usually ok, since you end up lying in bed.

What's terrifying is when a gaping hole opens up in real life and you say to yourself "I guess I'll be jumping into that before too long". And you've told yourself that it's a gaping hole and you know it's dangerous but then you catch yourself about to throw yourself into oblivion.

Without a rope. It's ok to throw yourself into oblivion if you know you've got a good rope. I guess I'll have to find one of those in the next year and a half.
